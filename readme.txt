The game is called Breakout or Brick Breaker. The goal of this game is to hit or break all the blocks with the ball, by moving the paddle on the bottom of the screen to the left or right directions. Player can move the paddle with the left and right key. To start the game, the player can either press space bar, or click the "Start/Pause" on the right top corner of the window. Make sure not to drop the ball, and always hit it with the paddle. If you let the ball fall, you will lose the game. For every block that you hit, you will get score of 10 points. A feature that is added to this game is that the player can pause the game by clicking the "Start/Pause" on the right top corner, or by pressing the space bar.
